APOLLON DATA METRICS (AGY) | Custom Facebook Page Requirements

Certain items are required for Facebook Pages. The agency needs to know this information in order to create an effective Facebook Page for your business and/or organization. These items, are as follows..

[+] title
[+] subtitle
[+] description
[+] icon
[+] banner
[+] website URL
[+] state
[+] city
[+] phone number
[+] email
[+] services list
[+] name (owner)
